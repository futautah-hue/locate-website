Our story
Built for people who explore
Every traveller knows the feeling. You step out of your hotel in a city you don't know, you turn left, then right, then left again, and twenty minutes later you have no idea where you are.

Locate was built to fix that. A simple flag at your starting point, a quiet trail recording in your pocket, and a way home whenever you need it.

From that beginning, Locate has grown into a travel community — a place to share what you find, follow people who go where you want to go, and connect with someone who actually lives where you're thinking of moving.

We are Balo Services — building tools that help people explore the world with confidence.

Request Account Deletion

Your data. Your choice.

What happens when you delete your account: Your profile, posts, stories, comments, and direct messages are permanently deleted from our servers. Map and trail data stored on your device is not affected — you can delete it from within the app or by uninstalling. We retain only what we are legally required to keep (e.g. transaction records for advertiser payments). Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

How to request deletion

Fill in the form below and we will process your request within 30 days. You will receive a confirmation email when deletion is complete. If you have trouble with the form, email us directly at locate@locatecommunity.com with the subject Account Deletion Request.

By submitting this form you confirm you are the account holder. Deletion is permanent and cannot be reversed.

Frequently asked questions

Can I pause my account instead?
Yes — you can deactivate your account temporarily from Settings inside the app without deleting your data.

What about my map and trail data?
Trail data is stored only on your device and is never sent to our servers. To remove it, delete the app from your device.

How long does deletion take?
We process all requests within 30 days. Cached copies on content delivery networks may persist for up to 90 days before expiring.

Can I create a new account after deleting?
Yes, using the same or a different email address.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 17 May 2026 · Effective: 17 May 2026

Plain-English summary: Locate is a travel, navigation, and social community app. Your map and trip data stays on your device. Your social profile, posts, stories, and messages are stored on our secure servers. We show paid advertisements in the social feed. We never sell your personal data.

1. Who We Are

Locate is developed and operated by Balo Services. Contact: locate@locatecommunity.com

2. Age Requirements

Minimum age:
• 13 years old — most countries worldwide
• 16 years old — Australia, South Korea, Germany, Austria, France, Belgium, Denmark, Netherlands, Norway, Finland

By creating an account you confirm you meet the minimum age for your country. We collect your date of birth and country at registration and enforce the minimum age automatically.

4. Background Location

Used solely for trail recording when screen is off. A persistent notification is shown while active. You can decline or revoke at any time in device Settings → Apps → Locate → Permissions.

5. Advertisements

Locate displays paid advertisements in the social feed, clearly labelled "Sponsored." We use only your profile country for targeting — no behavioural profiling, no third-party ad networks, no tracking pixels. Ads are not shown in the map and navigation section.

7. Your Rights

You can edit your profile, delete posts, withdraw location permission, and request a copy of your data at any time. To permanently delete your account and all associated data, use our Account Deletion Request page. For other data requests email locate@locatecommunity.com — we respond within 30 days.

8. Content Standards and Moderation

All content posted to Locate — including photos, videos, stories, and comments — is subject to our Community Guidelines set out in the Terms of Use. We do not permit the public sharing of graphic violence in any form outside of a clearly identified, genuine newsworthy context. Content that violates our guidelines is removed and may result in account suspension. Reported content data (report reason, content type, outcome) is retained for safety and audit purposes.

9. Children's Privacy

The map and navigation features contain no advertisements and store all data on device only. Social features are available to users meeting the minimum age. Parents who believe a child has created an account should email locate@locatecommunity.com immediately.
